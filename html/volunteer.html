STUDENT VOLUNTEERS:

Students can register themselves as student volunters. They can assist the affected personals. As a student volunteer you will be alterted only during small scale disasters to ensure student's safety. Any student under the age of 21 and above the age of 18.

MEDICAL VOLUNTEERS:

Medical staff, medical students, people who are certified to do first aid can sign up under medical volunteers. It is always better to have people who can help the paramedics to save as many lifes as possible.

RELIEF VOLUNTEERS:

Any citizen of India above the age of 21 can sign up under relief volunteers. The major job of these volunteers is to collect, pack and transport the relief items like food, clothes, shawls to the affected personals.

DIGITAL VOLUNTEERS:

Influencers, actors or people with large following base can sign up under digital volunteers. Social media can be very useful for creating awarness among people about the disaster occurred and how they can help.
